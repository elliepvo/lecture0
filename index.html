Eggplants

Although purple is the general color that comes to mind when the word eggplant is used, purple is not the only color of eggplants. Like all plants, eggplants come in different cultivars of shape, size, and color. First recorded in 1763, the word itself eggplant refers to the white cultivars, which look like a chicken's egg.

Hence the egg in eggplant.
